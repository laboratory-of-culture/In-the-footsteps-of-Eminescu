How it works

Just choose one of six Eminescu's place, press icon "View in AR" and look through the camera to find animated poetry text in the sky and urban landscape; it will be accompanied by the voice.

Explore the places

Turn on camera and put on your headphones

Walk his route, immerse yourself in augmented reality and feel partially involved in Eminescu life

Public presentation of project

In Chernivtsi, an AR application "In the Footsteps of Eminescu" was created. Thus, thanks to augmented reality, fragments of his poems "come to life" near the places connected with the poet.

This project was developed in cooperation with OpenHub Creative Cluster.
